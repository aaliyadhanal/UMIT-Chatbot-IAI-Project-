==================== UMIT COLLEGE INFORMATION ====================

===== GENERAL INFORMATION =====
College Name: Usha Mittal Institute of Technology (UMIT)
University: SNDT Women’s University
Location: Juhu, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Type: Private Engineering College for Women
Established: 1997
Approved by: AICTE

UMIT is one of the top women's engineering colleges in Mumbai and is known for quality technical education and placements.

===== COURSES OFFERED =====

Undergraduate (B.Tech):
- Computer Engineering
- Information Technology
- Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
- Data Science

Postgraduate:
- M.Tech (varies by year and specialization)

===== ADMISSION PROCESS =====

Admissions are based on:
- MHT-CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test)
- JEE Main (All India candidates)

Process:
- Centralized Admission Process (CAP)
- Students must have completed 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics

===== FEES STRUCTURE =====

- Tuition Fees: Approx ₹1,00,000 – ₹1,50,000 per year
- Additional Fees: Exam fees, library fees, development charges

(Note: Fees may change every year)
